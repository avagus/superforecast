Methodology

We estimate the probability of specific geopolitical outcomes using a base rate and multiplicative Bayes Factors (BFs) across six buckets: Military, Economy/Energy, Elite/Institutions, Domestic/Public, External/Diplomatic, and Symbolic/Context.

  • Controls: correlation penalty (0.90–0.95), one-BF-per-bucket-per-cycle, and caps for Economy/Energy to prevent double-counting.
  • Standards: BF > 1.15 requires ≥2 independent reputable sources or hard data (imagery/filings).
  • Posterior: O = O₀ × Π(BFᵢ); P = O/(1+O).

Updates are weekly; inflection notes are posted ad hoc.
